Bike Hiring

ID is required to hire our bikes. A passport or drivers licence along with a credit card deposit. Over 18’s must sign for and supervise under 16’s. 16 and 17-year-olds can ride unsupervised but cannot supervise under 16’s. All bikes are to be returned/collected by 5pm or a second day will be charged.
